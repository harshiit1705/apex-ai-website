Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

How long does implementation take?

Most workflows go from kickoff to a working automation within days, not months — since each build targets one specific process rather than a full platform overhaul. Complexity and integrations affect the exact timeline.

Do I need technical knowledge?

No. You explain the process you want automated, and the technical setup, testing, and maintenance are handled for you. You only need to know your own business process.

Can AI work with my existing systems?

In most cases, yes. Automations are typically built to connect with tools you already use — like your CRM, calendar, spreadsheets, or messaging platforms — rather than asking you to switch systems.

How much can automation save?

It depends on the process, but repetitive tasks like lead follow-up, scheduling, and answering common questions are typically reduced by a significant margin once handled automatically. We'll give you a realistic estimate during your free consultation rather than a generic number.

Why should I trust you with this?

Fair question — here's the honest answer. This is a founder-led studio, so you work directly with the person actually building your automation, not a sales team handing it off to someone else. Every workflow shown on this site is real, demonstrable, and built using the same tools and logic that would power your system. There's no pressure or upfront cost to start: the first consultation is free, and you'll see exactly how the automation works — step by step — before deciding anything. You're welcome to ask detailed technical questions about how a workflow is built; that transparency is the trust.
